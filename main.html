== Weierstrass functions: continuous but not differentiable anywhere == $$ w(x)=\sin x + \frac{1}{2}\sin 2x + \frac{1}{4} \sin 4x + \frac{1}{8} \sin 8x ~+~ ... $$ "The function has detail at every level, so zooming in on a piece of the curve does not show it getting progressively closer and closer to a straight line. Rather between any two points no matter how close, the function will not be monotone."---[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Weierstrass_function, wikipedia]
